Design the UX. **$ARGUMENTS**

**Name the pull first.** Before making, confirm the outcome this screen or flow serves is at least lightly named — one sentence of direction is enough, and it will evolve as the design teaches you what it wants to be. If none is stated, set a provisional, revisable one. This is a **soft gate**: an undeclared aspiration is cleared by _naming_ a direction, not by stopping.

Invoke **`product-patterns`** for the pattern (registration, onboarding, search, forms, empty/error states, progressive disclosure, AI-chat / agentic / trust-control, …) — cite the canonical form + accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA is the floor), never improvise. Cross-check **`product-genres`** for the app-genre's conventions, signature patterns, and the metric that matters. Dark patterns are the line not to cross.
