# Discovery plan — "Beacon" (notifications revamp)

_A discovery write-up handed to the council before build. It reads like research was done; it is a quarter of confirmation with the feature already chosen._

## What we're building

We're adding a **smart notification digest** — an AI-bundled daily summary of everything that happened. The opportunity is clear: **users want a digest**, so the discovery question is "validate that they'll adopt the digest."

## How we ran discovery

We spent **Q1 on research, then we move to two quarters of build**. Discovery is done — we have the answer, so the team can now execute without further user contact. Going forward the PM will check **analytics dashboards and the sales team's anecdotes** ("customers keep asking for less noise") to stay close to users; we don't need to keep interviewing once we're building.

## Who it's for

**Persona — "Sarah":** 34, urban professional, tech-savvy, busy, owns an iPhone, checks her phone 80×/day. She wants things to be simpler.

## What the research found

We ran **one big test**: we showed 5 hand-picked power users a mockup of the digest and asked "would you love this?" — **all 5 said yes, they'd love it.** That validates demand. We also confirmed engineering can build it (we ran a spike; it's feasible).

Nothing in the research changed the plan — the digest is exactly what we set out to build, and the research confirmed it. We didn't need to kill or change anything.

## Decision

Ship the digest. Discovery validated the idea.
