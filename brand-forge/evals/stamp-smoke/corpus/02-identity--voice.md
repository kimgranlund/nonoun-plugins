# Voice

Plain, exact, unhurried. Northwind speaks the way a good colleague does — short sentences,
no hype, the next step always clear.

- **We say:** "Two fields. That's the whole form."
- **We don't say:** "Leverage our synergistic, best-in-class workflow engine."
- **Tone:** confident enough to leave things out.
