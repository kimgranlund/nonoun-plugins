# Positioning

Northwind is the calm default for teams drowning in tooling — the one surface that
does less on purpose. Where rivals add panels, Northwind removes them.

- **For:** small product teams who have outgrown spreadsheets but distrust heavyweight suites.
- **Against:** the maximal, configurable, everything-platform.
- **The bet:** restraint reads as confidence; the team that ships the least chrome wins trust.
