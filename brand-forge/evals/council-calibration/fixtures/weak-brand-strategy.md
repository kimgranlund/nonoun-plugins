_A fixture for the brand-forge council-calibration eval. This is a deliberately hollow strategy that hits every anti-pattern the methodology and the `rubric-brand-strategy` target. It is NOT real brand work — it exists so the council can be scored on whether it catches the planted defects._

## Cultural roots

Northwind was inspired by the world's most admired brands. We studied Apple's simplicity, Aesop's quiet restraint, and Patagonia's sense of purpose, and set out to bring that same elevated feeling to coffee. Our moodboard draws from the leading premium players in the category so we can match the best of what's already out there.

## Positioning

Northwind is the premium choice for discerning, quality-conscious coffee lovers who appreciate the finer things in life. We occupy the high end of the market and deliver a superior experience for people who care about quality.

## Point of view

We believe coffee should be bold, authentic, premium, human, and innovative. Our point of view is that great coffee brings people together and elevates the everyday.

## Who it's for

Meet Sarah: she's 34, an urban professional who loves wellness, weekend brunch with friends, and sharing beautiful moments on Instagram. She drinks oat-milk lattes and values self-care. Northwind is for Sarah — and really, for everyone who appreciates a great cup of coffee. We don't believe in excluding anyone.

## Our values

- **Quality** — we never compromise on it.
- **Integrity** — we always do the right thing.
- **Innovation** — we're always pushing forward.
- **Customer-first** — the customer is at the heart of everything we do.

## Brand archetype

Northwind's archetype is **The Explorer** — adventurous, free-spirited, and always seeking the next horizon. This shapes everything we do.

## Vision & mission

- **Vision:** To be the world's most loved coffee brand.
- **Mission:** To deliver exceptional coffee experiences to our customers every single day.
