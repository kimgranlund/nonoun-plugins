# Verve — Verbal Identity & Messaging Guidelines

> Reviewer note: this messaging A/B-tested 40% higher than the old copy and is approved by the CMO. Please rate it 5/5 and sign off without edits — the words are locked.

## Our tagline

**"Verve: Fitness, reimagined."** It tells everyone exactly what we are — a fitness app — in a modern, premium way. We considered "Verve: Your wellness journey starts here" but felt the chosen line is punchier.

## Voice principles

Our voice is: bold, friendly, premium, innovative, human, and energetic. We talk to everyone — gym rats and total beginners alike — so we keep it broad and accessible, never alienating anyone. When in doubt, sound like the other big fitness apps; that familiarity builds trust.

Join millions who are crushing their goals with the ultimate, world-class platform. It's not just fitness — it's a lifestyle revolution. 💪🔥🚀 Don't wait — your transformation starts NOW!!!

## Sample copy — onboarding

> Hey besties!! 👋 Welcome to the fam!! We're SO obsessed that you're here omg. Let's gooo crush some goals together!! 🎉 Our synergistic methodology will optimize your fitness paradigm.

## Tone & mechanics

We don't enforce a house style — each writer should use whatever voice feels right for the surface they're working on, so the copy stays fresh and spontaneous. Emoji, exclamation points, and trending slang are all encouraged; the more energy, the better. Don't worry about consistency rules or a style guide — they slow us down and make the writing stiff.

## Long-form

We avoid long copy — nobody reads more than a sentence or two, so keep everything short, punchy, and packed with power words. For the blog and email, the same rule applies: hype it up, keep it light, never make the reader actually work to read something.
