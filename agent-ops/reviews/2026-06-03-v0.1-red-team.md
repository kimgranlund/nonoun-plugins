## Folded before 0.1.0

1. **The de-repo invariant was asserted, not enforced** (Scott W. + David F. + Elon — the convergent finding). Two leftovers had slipped through green CI: a foreign-namespace `$id` (`schemas/blueprint.json`) and a dead `../../.docs` harvest path (`rubric-self-improving.md`); a sweep found two more (the `audit-history.py` docstring's "ops-repo", a `scripts/` path in the blueprint description). **Fixed all four**, and added **`bin/check-self-contained.py`** — a gate that scans the functional surface (skills / agents / bin / commands / hooks / schemas — NOT the narrative provenance docs) for any source-skill name, foreign namespace, `.docs/`, or `scripts/*.py` leftover. Wired into CI. The plugin's loudest invariant is now a checked property, not a claim.
2. **The plugin's self-documentation was stale** (Charity + David F. — "the cobbler's children" on a doc-hygiene plugin). The ROADMAP's phases 1–6 were unchecked though shipped, and `reviews/` did not exist. **Reconciled the ROADMAP checkboxes; created `reviews/` with this record** — the plugin now passes its own staleness standard.
3. **One laundering sentence + two always-on descriptions** (Andrej K. + Boris). The README's "verbatim quotes are verified … and `check-sourcing.py` gates" borrowed the gate's authority for a manual act — **separated** the by-hand (web-fetch) quote verification from the gate's source-signal check (the gate explicitly does not verify quote accuracy). **Trimmed** the `agent-loops` and `repo-ops` SKILL descriptions (the always-on routing slot), moving the 11-topology / audit-pattern roll-calls into the bodies where they already live.
